Welcome to the Animal World

Animals are living beings that inhabit almost every part of the Earth. They come in various shapes, sizes, and species.

Popular Animals

  • Lion – Known as the king of the jungle.
  • Elephant – The largest land animal.
  • Dolphin – An intelligent marine mammal.
  • Dog – A loyal companion to humans.
  • Cat – A playful and independent pet.

Why Animals Matter

Animals play a crucial role in maintaining balance in nature. They are part of the ecosystem and help in pollination, seed dispersal, and maintaining food chains.
